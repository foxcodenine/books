Briards

A Heart wrapped in fur

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditiomally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to gave originated in France, has been vred for certuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currenly the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napolean, Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned brtards.
